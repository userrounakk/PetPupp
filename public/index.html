Our Impact

Every year, over a thousand street dogs die in Nepal as a result of illness, disease, or accident. The ONLY solutions for sustinableÂ street dogs management are proper medical care, population control, and the creation of a healthy environment. Our impact is to reveal suffering, rescue dogs in need, and protect them. Our vision is a world in which humans treat dogs with integrity, empathy, and understanding.

Who are We?

We are a small group of animal lovers and tech enthusiasts dreaming of making a better life for our beautiful yet abandoned creatures through our platform PetPUpp . PetPupp is a non-profit platform that aims to protect streets dogs and animals from torture, cruelty, and ill-usage of any kind.
